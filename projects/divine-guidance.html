Project Overview

Divine Guidance is a Progressive Web Application (PWA) designed to offer accessible, personalized spiritual support. In a world where mental well-being and spiritual connection are increasingly sought after, this application bridges the gap between traditional scripture and modern personalized assistance.

Users can input their current life worries or questions, and the system intelligently analyzes their sentiment to provide targeted Biblical verses and compassionate, AI-generated homilies. This offers users immediate comfort and actionable spiritual direction tailored specifically to their situation.

Key Features

Personalized Scripture

Leverages natural language processing to understand the nuance of user queries and retrieve the most relevant Bible verses for their specific context.

AI-Generated Homilies

Generates short, compassionate sermons (homilies) on-demand, explaining how the selected scripture applies to the user's current life challenges.

Progressive Web App (PWA)

Built as a PWA to ensure offline capability, fast load times, and an "installable" app-like experience on mobile devices without needing an app store.
